History

Classmate, a brand under ITC Limited, was introduced in 2003. It started with a mission to deliver superior-quality stationery products for students in India.

Over the years, Classmate has grown to become one of the most trusted names in education, providing innovative and sustainable products for students of all ages.

  • Industry: Consumer goods
  • Founded: 2003; 21 years ago
  • Headquarters: Delhi, India
  • Area served: India
  • Key people: Ayaan Ali (CEO)
  • Products:
    1. Notebooks
    2. Pens
    3. Pencils
    4. Math instruments
    5. Erasers
    6. Pencil sharpeners
